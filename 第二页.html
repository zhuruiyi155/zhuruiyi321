BEST OF 2024
pitchfork
If 2023 was the year of the record-breaking tour, then 2024 was the year of the blockbuster album, as some of the biggest names in music came back with marquee releases. Even the Cure decided it was time to return from hibernation. Still, as ever, there was room for newcomers and underground darlings to stake their claim among the giants. And, ultimately, the year may end up best remembered for hip-hop’s greatest feud in decades. It’s now time to consider those albums, songs, and the thousands beyond them to bring you our package celebrating the best music of 2024. Join us as we revisit the defining sounds from rock, rap, electronic, and more, plus, the best books, merchandise, and more. Curious about what our readers were listening to? Be sure to see the results of our Readers’ Poll. Happy reading, and happy listening!
